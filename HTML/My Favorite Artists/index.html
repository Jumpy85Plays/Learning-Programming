My Favorite Artists

Calliope Mori

Mori Calliope is a talented music producer and rapper, known for her work as a virtual YouTuber under hololive, a popular VTuber agency. Debuting as part of hololive English -Myth- in 2020, she has garnered a large following for her unique blend of English and Japanese lyrics, as well as her distinctive style that merges elements of rap, hip-hop, and other musical genres. Mori Calliope's music often features introspective themes and a captivating narrative style, showcasing her versatility and creativity as an artist. She is also recognized for her dynamic performances and engaging personality, making her a standout figure in the VTuber community.
Ado

Ado is a rising Japanese singer known for her powerful and versatile voice. She gained widespread recognition with her debut single "Usseewa," which became a massive hit and showcased her distinctive vocal style and rebellious, energetic persona. Ado's music often features bold, expressive lyrics and a mix of rock, pop, and other contemporary genres. Despite her young age, she has quickly become a prominent figure in the Japanese music scene, admired for her vocal prowess and dynamic performances. Ado's unique sound and charismatic presence have earned her a rapidly growing fanbase both in Japan and internationally.
Will Stetson

Will Stetson is an American singer-songwriter and music producer known for his emotive vocal style and genre-blending sound. His music often combines elements of pop, rock, and indie, characterized by introspective lyrics and dynamic arrangements. Stetson's work reflects a deep passion for storytelling and personal expression, which resonates strongly with listeners. With a growing reputation in the music industry, he continues to capture audiences with his authentic voice and innovative approach to music.
OR30

OR30 is a Japanese music producer and composer known for his work in the vocaloid and J-pop genres. He gained prominence through his innovative use of vocaloid technology, creating complex and emotionally resonant tracks that often feature intricate arrangements and a unique blend of electronic and traditional elements. OR30's music is distinguished by its rich, atmospheric soundscapes and the ability to evoke a wide range of emotions, earning him a dedicated following among fans of digital and contemporary music.
